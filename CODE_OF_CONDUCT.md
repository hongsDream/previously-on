# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respectful feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing
on what is best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized language
or attention, trolling, insults, threats, publishing private information, or other conduct that
would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through the repository maintainer's private contact path described
in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), without including unrelated vulnerability details. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban participants for
conduct they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
